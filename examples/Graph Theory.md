# Graph Theory

The study of **graphs** — structures of *vertices* connected by *edges*. Graphs
model networks, dependencies, maps, and social relationships.

- **Directed** vs **undirected** edges
- **Weighted** vs unweighted
- Common traversals: BFS, DFS

Shortest-path algorithms such as [[Dijkstra's Algorithm]] and [[Bellman-Ford]]
build directly on these foundations. #graphs
